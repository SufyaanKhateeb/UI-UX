Monochrome Signal

A monochrome signal is a type of signal that consists of a single color or shade, often used in various applications such as television broadcasting, photography, and digital imaging. It can represent different intensities of light or color in a simplified manner. Designed to convey information without the complexity of multiple colors, monochrome signals are often used in technical fields and artistic expressions.
